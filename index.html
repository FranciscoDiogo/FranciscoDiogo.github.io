I am a Post-Doc Researcher at LIP in Lisbon, Portugal as a member of the Pierre Auger collaboration.

I work on high-energy particle astrophysics, in particular trying to understand the composition of the most energetic particles in the universe measured at the Pierre Auger Observatory. This was the subject of my PhD Thesis, whcih you can find here.

I am also a founding member of TreeTree2, a non-profit organization whose aim is improving the intelectual development of young students. This starts by asking the question "What would you like to learn?", and bringing together the students and professional mentors in their chosen fields. More info and additional projects can be found here (in portuguese only).
